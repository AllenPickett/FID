Steamed Broccoli

This is a really simple dish to prepare and it's always a big hit at parties. Friends from our last event said:

“It's healthy and delicious!”

Ingredients

  • 4 broccoli heads
  • 2 large garlic cloves
  • 1tbs salt/pepper
  • 1 t. capers
  • 1/3 cup water

Instructions

  1. Combine capers, salt/pepper, and garlic in a food processor. Blend until as smooth as possible.
  2. Bring water to a boil in a medium pot
  3. Coat the freshly rinced broccoli in seasoning
  4. Add to boiling water covering with lid for 5-8 minutes
  5. Strain water from broccoli and serve warm
